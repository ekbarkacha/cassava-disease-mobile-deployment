Privacy Policy for CassavaCare

Effective date: December 2025

1. Introduction

CassavaCare ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

CassavaCare processes images of cassava leaves locally on your device for disease detection. No personal data, such as your name, email, or location, is collected or transmitted to any server.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information processed by the app is used solely to detect cassava crop diseases and provide remedies. All scan results are stored securely on your device.

4. Data Storage

Your scan history remains on your device and is not shared externally. We recommend regular backups if you wish to retain your scan data permanently.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not send data to any third-party analytics or tracking services. All processing occurs locally.

6. Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect your scan data on the device. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.
